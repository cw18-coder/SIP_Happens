The MF Stack - Understanding the Basics

Mutual Funds: The Microservices of Investment

Like microservices architecture, mutual funds break down the complex task of stock picking into manageable, specialized components.

👨‍💼

Fund Manager = Lead Developer

Makes the technical decisions and architectural choices. Just like you trust your lead developer to make technology decisions, you trust fund managers to make investment decisions based on their expertise.

  • ✓ Responsibilities
  • ✓ Experience
  • ✓ Strategy
📊

Portfolio = Codebase

Collection of stocks and bonds working together harmoniously. A well-designed portfolio, like good code, is maintainable, scalable, and performs consistently under different market conditions.

  • 📊 Diversified
  • ⚡ Scalable
  • 🔧 Maintainable
🔢

NAV = Version Number

Daily updates showing current value. Unlike software versions that you control, NAV updates happen automatically based on market performance.

  • 📅 Daily Updates
  • 📈 Market Driven
  • 👁️ Transparent
💰

Expense Ratio = Azure Bills

The cost of running your investment infrastructure. Just like cloud costs, you want efficiency without compromising performance.

  • 💰 Annual Cost
  • ⚡ Efficiency
  • 🎯 Optimization
🚀
Key Difference: Unlike your production server, mutual funds are designed to scale UP, not crash at 3 AM! Though market volatility can feel like getting paged during a weekend deploy.

Speaker Notes:

  • Use familiar tech analogies to explain MF concepts
  • Emphasize the professional management aspect
  • Connect to their daily work experience
  • Make the 3 AM server crash joke - gets good laughs
  • Time: 5-6 minutes
